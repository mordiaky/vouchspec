# VouchSpec autonomous commercial operation goal

Continue operating the public Stage A VouchSpec service while proving genuine, unrelated
external adoption and a repeatable autonomous business. The goal is achieved only when all
30 tests in `GOAL_EVIDENCE.md` pass, including:

- three retained publisher integrations in repositories controlled by three unrelated owners;
- 100 legitimate external machine requests from at least ten unrelated sources;
- 20 non-burst repeat requests from at least five of those sources;
- a complete machine-readable pay-per-use purchase and delivery flow;
- three unrelated settled buyers, at least USD $500 settled gross external revenue, and one
  repeat paid buyer;
- positive contribution margin with every variable cost recorded and no more than USD $100
  lifetime owner-funded spending;
- 14 consecutive days of autonomous operation after the first settled payment;
- repeatable acquisition, paid fulfillment, and self-serve support; and
- an independent final evidence audit.

Operator activity, owned demos, monitoring, smoke/load tests, related parties, test payments,
pending/reversed/refunded payments, and agent-created demand never count toward commercial
acceptance.

The product boundary remains immutable public-source static inspection. Arbitrary uploads,
private repositories, confidential content, and artifact execution are outside this goal.
