# Initial operating goal

Generate at least **USD $500 in settled gross revenue** from at least three genuine,
unaffiliated paying customers while:

- delivering every purchased CapabilityProof product;
- maintaining positive contribution margin;
- keeping owner-funded spending at or below USD $100;
- operating a repeatable acquisition and fulfillment process; and
- passing an independent evidence audit.

The first milestone is narrower: demonstrate a reproducible, non-executing verification
pipeline that produces a deterministic Capability Receipt for a real public `SKILL.md`
artifact and exposes the same result through CLI, HTTP, and MCP-compatible interfaces.
