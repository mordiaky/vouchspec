# Pricing hypotheses

| Product | Initial price | Evidence status |
|---|---:|---|
| Public search | Free | 25-entry public catalog live; external usage unmeasured |
| Existing signed receipt retrieval | Free | Public exact-byte retrieval live; external usage unmeasured |
| Receipt comparison | USD $0.01 | Planned machine-native rail only; not orderable |
| Fresh public static validation | **USD $49.00** | Request/quote preview implemented; worker and payment rail not live |
| Signed receipt issuance | Included in fresh validation initially | Not separately orderable |
| Continuous public-artifact monitoring | USD $2-$25 monthly | Deferred until integration/payment/request evidence |

The $49 fresh-validation price is an unvalidated launch hypothesis, not a claimed market
price. The prior $0.10 hypothesis was below Stripe's $0.50 USD minimum and could not absorb
its fixed processing fee. At $49, an illustrative US domestic card fee of 2.9% + $0.30 is
$1.72. With a hard direct-fulfillment-cost ceiling of $5, illustrative contribution is
$42.28 before refunds or disputes. Actual provider fees and every direct cost must be
reconciled per order.

Eleven non-refunded $49 orders would produce $539 gross, but only genuine unrelated-party
payments that become available at the provider count toward settled revenue. Test mode,
owner funds, related accounts, refunds, disputes, promotional credits, and pending balances
never count.

The service returns a machine-readable quote before payment. Payment never changes evidence
labels, ranking, findings, or severity.
