# Pricing hypotheses

| Product | Current price | Evidence status |
|---|---:|---|
| Public catalog search | Free | 25-entry catalog live; external use unmeasured |
| Existing signed receipt retrieval | Free | Exact bytes are public, cacheable, and shareable |
| Live receipt invalidation status | Free | Separate no-store status resource is live |
| Testnet fresh public static validation | **1.00 test USDC** | Public x402 Base Sepolia sandbox; never revenue |
| Commercial fresh public static validation | **0.25 USDC** | Researched launch cohort; unvalidated by buyers; mainnet disabled |
| Signed receipt issuance | Included | Delivered automatically by the sandbox |
| Continuous monitoring | Not priced | Deferred pending adoption and payment evidence |

The public sandbox is faucet-sized so autonomous agents can prove the complete HTTP 402,
settlement, fulfillment, signing, verification, caching, and invalidation flow without creating
revenue. It is not a discount, promotion, buyer, or commercial transaction.

The earlier $49 commercial hypothesis is rejected. A complete 26,623-item Coinbase Bazaar sample
retained 12,360 deduplicated Base-mainnet USDC accepts; 97.45% were below $1 and the 95th
percentile was $0.30. Semantically close paid operations clustered from $0.10 to $1.00, while
free scanners and native attestations constrain willingness to pay. The detailed method,
comparables, limits, and sources are in
`research/agent-only-pricing-analysis-2026-07-14.md`.

The first genuine mainnet cohort is fixed at 0.25 USDC. A conservative $0.05 variable-cost reserve
leaves $0.20 contribution, or 80%, before actual cost data exists. Test $0.10 only after 14 days
with qualified exposure through three unrelated integrations and zero genuine settlements. Test
$0.50 only after at least three unrelated buyers, one repeat buyer, positive contribution, and a
remedy rate below 5%; otherwise hold. The service will not count revenue until an unrelated
buyer's mainnet payment is settled and neither refunded, reversed, disputed, owner-funded, nor
related-party funded. Payment never changes evidence labels, ranking, findings, or severity.
